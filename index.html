Our Project Process

From initial consultation to project completion, our proven process ensures clear communication, quality work, and results that exceed your expectations.

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your project vision, budget, and timeline. We take time to understand exactly what you want to achieve with your remodel or construction project.

2

Planning & Estimates

Detailed planning phase with clear project scope, material selection, and transparent pricing. We offer both time & material and bid-type project options.

3

Permits & Scheduling

We handle necessary permits and coordinate with our trusted network of subcontractors to ensure your project stays on schedule.

4

Construction & Communication

Quality hands-on work with regular updates. We maintain open communication throughout the project and address any changes promptly.

5

Completion & Follow-up

Final walkthrough, project cleanup, and ongoing support. We stand behind our work and maintain relationships with our clients.

About Bill Artukovich

I started my construction business in 1999, initially focusing on hard surface flooring installation including hardwood, tile, and laminate. From there, I expanded into commercial building across the US in the action sports industry and salon construction.

Here in Montana, I've focused on residential new building and remodeling projects. Over the years, I've built a great network of trusted subcontractors and would be happy to be a part of your next project. My approach emphasizes efficiency, quality workmanship, and keeping projects within budget and on schedule.
